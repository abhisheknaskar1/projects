The Impact of Sanctions on Businesses

Direct Legal Consequences

Asset freezes of any company holdings in the United States

Prohibition on business dealings between American citizens or organizations with sanctioned companies

Blocking of US-based assets owned by the sanctioned entities

Enforcement actions against personnel involved in sanctioned transactions

International Business Impact

Restrictions on accessing international markets 

Limited ability to participate in global value chains, especially for network products like computers and electronics 

Damage to business reputation and relationships with Western partners

Reduced export potential, particularly in sectors connected to global supply chains

Compliance Failures

Companies must implement robust compliance frameworks to avoid sanctions

Need for careful structuring of international business dealings

Requirement to demonstrate compliance with applicable regulations

Necessity to respond to inquiries from Western regulators/p>
